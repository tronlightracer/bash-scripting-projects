chmod +x adds executable permisions to the user, group and everyone else
chmod -x removes executable permisions to the user, group and everyone else
chmod -xwr removes executable, write and read permisions to the file

numerical:
4 = read, 2 = write, 1 = executable
744 would give read, write and executable permisions to the user while the group and everyone else just has read permisions

777 would give read, write and executable permisions to the user, group and everyone else
